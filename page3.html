Tale of Two Squares

My aim for this project was not to create a narrative with two squares but to instead present a highly resolved animation of After Effects techniques.

Unlike my initial version of "The Tale of Two Squares", I explored with a lot of visual research and turorials to learn new skills and techniques. I found the task immensely difficult as I constantly struggled with keyframes and learning concepts such as "squashing" and "stretching". It is not a task that I would re-do for a second time however I am glad about the effort I put into it. Unlike my first attempt, I did many iterations and focused on created squares with a smooth and elegant flow.
