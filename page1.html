Kris Andrew Small's Masterclass

With his explosive colour and illustrative style, Kris Andrew Small lead a masterclass that transformed slogans into a creative art style. Softwares used were Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, and scanned textures.

I found the entire process to be extremely iterative and fun. I valued how Kris would commentate through his mark making methods because it made me realise the joy and spontaneous nature that he channelled. My main three works presented accumulate the fun that I had however I believe that if I spent more time reworking aspects and gathering more visual research, they could be further refined.
