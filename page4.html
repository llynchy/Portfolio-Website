Typographic Layout

This was created by printing out strips that said ‘responsibilty of ethics’. I then cut them up and then placed them onto a scanner. While the image was being scanned, I carefully moved the strips, this gave the warped effect.

These three pages presented are the final design of the article. I am glad that I didnt stop where I thought I finished the project because the title page works a lot better in this spread. This new title page was directly inspired by my previous research of Tonny Cragg’s design. The whole process was diffcult and tedious at times, especially working with rags, however I am very pleased with the final outcome.
